Welcome to the Japanese Students Association (JSA)

Explore the beauty of Japanese culture, engage in exciting events, and become a part of a welcoming community at the University of New Hampshire!

What is JSA?

The Japanese Students Association (JSA) aims to bring together students interested in learning about Japanese culture, language, and traditions. We host various cultural events and activities throughout the year!

Why Join JSA?

Joining JSA offers opportunities to engage with others who share your passion for Japanese culture. Whether you're learning Japanese or just curious, JSA is a community you'll love to be a part of!
